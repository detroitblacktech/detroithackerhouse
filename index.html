Why Detroit Hacker House

A Team Creates Amazing Work for Your Startup

Most programs teach you about startups. We put you in the room to build one. Our model removes the barriers that slow founders down – cost of living, isolation, access to mentors, and the gap between learning and doing.

Founders in the House live together, build together, and ship together. The result: real products, real traction, and founders prepared for what comes next.

View Program
How it works

Build > Ship > Learn

Our program is built around one principle: you learn by doing. Every founder in the House is expected to ship – not someday, but now.

MVP Development

We coach you through building a minimum viable product and getting it in front of real customers. No theory. Real validation.

Mentorship

Work directly with our House Manager and Entrepreneurs in Residence through workshops, office hours, and one-on-one support.

Housing

Live alongside other founders with free room, board, wifi, housekeeping, desk space, and team outings. No distractions.

Venture Backable

We align our program milestones with what investors actually look for – so when you pitch, you're ready.

The Space

Building bridges between ideas and execution

With dedicated workspace, high-speed wifi, and a community of builders around you, the House is designed to remove every barrier between you and shipping your product.

We evaluate founders not by their pitch decks, but by their willingness to build. If you're ready to ship, we're ready to support you.
