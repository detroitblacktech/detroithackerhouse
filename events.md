# Startup 101

Learn the basics of what it means to run a tech startup — from coming up with an idea to forming a team, building a product, and finding your first customers. If you’ve ever thought about launching your own idea, TechTown is your first stop on the journey into tech entrepreneurship, no matter your background!
Wayne State students will also get the inside scoop on our Live Innovate Play program and how to apply for our upcoming cohort.

You’ll walk away with an understanding of:

- What defines a tech-enabled business
-  Shaping your idea into a startup concept
-  Building the right team for your vision
-  Finding and connecting with customers
-  Local resources and next steps

# The Lean Startup Methodology

Learn about taking an iterative approach to building your startup using the Lean Startup Methodology by Eric Ries. Wayne State students will also get the inside scoop on our Live Innovate Play program and how to apply for our upcoming cohort.

You’ll walk away with an understanding of:

- Understanding the Build-Measure-Learn feedback loop
- Defining and testing your value hypothesis
- Identifying and measuring actionable metrics
- Learning when and how to pivot or persevere
- Applying validated learning to grow your startup
